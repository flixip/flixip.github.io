Reasons to Aging Population

Direct Driving Factors

Decline in Fertility Rate

  • The global total fertility rate has decreased from 4.7 (1950) to 2.3 (2023)
  • Extreme cases: South Korea 0.72 | China 1.07
  • Delayed marriage and childbearing: The average age of first marriage in China has increased from 22 to 28 years old (1990-2023)

Prolongation of Life Expectancy

  • The global average life expectancy has increased from 46 to 73 years old (1950-2023)
  • Japan and Switzerland have exceeded the 84-year-old threshold
  • Investment in health: The number of fitness enthusiasts has increased by 300% (2010-2023)

Structural Causes

The Price of Industrialization

  • The average family size has decreased from 5.3 to 2.6 people in China (1982-2020)
  • The empty-nest rate in rural areas is more than 60%

Women's Awakening

  • Women account for 52.5% of higher education enrollment
  • The employment rate of women aged 25-34 is 75% (South Korea)

Economic Squeeze

  • The cost of raising a child in first-tier cities: 800,000 yuan for 18 years
  • The housing price-to-income ratio in Shenzhen is 35:1

Cultural Transformation

  • The number of DINK (Double Income No Kids) families has exceeded 2 million in China
  • The number of elderly people living alone has increased threefold (2000-2020)
